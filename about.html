Background

I am a passionate Naval Architect with a strong background in Ocean Engineering and Naval Architecture. I am currently affiliated with the Department of Ocean Engineering and Naval Architecture at the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur.

Research Interests

My research interests include Naval Architecture, Fluid Structure Interaction, Nonlinear Dynamics, and Machine Learning applications in engineering. My work aims to contribute to innovative solutions that improve ship design and marine operations.

Education

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Naval Architecture and Ship Building and a Master's degree in Ocean Engineering. Throughout my academic journey, I have been driven by a curiosity to explore the dynamics of marine structures and their interactions with the environment.
